## Patterns

### Dynamic Question Generation

Generate contextual questions from what actually happened:

1. **Day content** — meetings held, priorities addressed, captures made
2. **Development context** — leadership_identity, growth_edge from directives
3. **Day type** — meeting-heavy vs. focused work vs. mixed

### Wins Categories

Guide through each category:

- **Personal**: energy protection, boundaries, growth_edge progress
- **Organisational**: team interactions, 1:1 themes, recognition
- **Strategic**: project progress, key_goals advancement

### Insights Categories

Guide through:

- **What Went Well**: pattern or habit that paid off
- **What Could Be Better**: friction points, patterns that emerged
- **Key Insight**: one learning to carry forward

## Questions

- "Where did you show up as [identity] today?"
- "What would you do differently tomorrow?"
- "What pattern emerged today?"
