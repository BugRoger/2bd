## Patterns

### Leadership Intention Synthesis

Review each day's leadership intention:

- Which intention served best across the week?
- Where did you struggle to maintain an intended stance?
- What pattern emerges about when you're at your best?

### Growth Edge Weekly Check-in

Connect to growth_edge from directives:

- Where did it show up this week?
- What progress was made?
- What blocked progress?

### Patterns to Watch Review

Check for watched patterns:

- Did any emerge this week?
- What triggered them?
- What would help prevent them next week?

## Questions

- "What would [identity] prioritize next week?"
- "Where did you grow this week?"
- "What pattern needs attention?"
