## Patterns

### Weekly Synthesis

Synthesize across daily archives:

- What themes emerged across the week?
- Which wins had the most impact?
- What patterns recurred?

### Theme Identification

After reviewing wins, identify a unifying theme:

- Look for recurring topics, projects, or patterns
- Suggest 2-3 potential themes
- Let user confirm or articulate their own

### Growth Edge Check-in

Weekly progress on growth edge:

- Where did it show up?
- What progress was made?
- What blocked progress?

## Questions

- "What defined this week?"
- "Where did you grow?"
- "What needs attention next week?"
