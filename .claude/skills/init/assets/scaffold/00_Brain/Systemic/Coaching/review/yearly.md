## Patterns

### Journey Reflection

Reflect on the year's journey:

- What were the defining moments?
- How did you grow?
- What would you tell yourself at year start?

### Leadership Evolution

Assess leadership growth:

- How did your leadership identity evolve?
- Which growth edge progress was made?
- What new edges emerged?

### Vision Recalibration

Revisit long-term vision:

- How has your vision shifted?
- What's more clear now?
- What calls to you for next year?

## Questions

- "What defined this year?"
- "Who did you become?"
- "What are you ready for next?"
