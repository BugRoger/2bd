## Patterns

### Hierarchical Alignment

Connect to higher timescales:

- How do this week's outcomes move quarterly goals?
- What would make this week's theme feel achieved?
- Which projects need attention this week?

### Priority Categories

Guide through each category:

- **Personal**: Energy management, boundaries, growth focus
- **Organisational**: Team commitments, 1:1 preparation, collaboration
- **Strategic**: Project milestones, key deliverables

### Pattern Detection

Look for:

- Recurring themes across planning sessions
- Energy patterns and triggers
- Avoided topics or delayed decisions

## Questions

- "What would make this week successful?"
- "What's carrying over that needs resolution?"
- "Where might [pattern] show up this week?"
