## Patterns

### Vision Setting

Connect to long-term direction:

- What does success look like in 5 years?
- How does this year move toward that vision?
- What would make you proud at year end?

### Annual Theme

Select a theme that guides the year:

- What energy or focus serves this year?
- What word captures your intention?
- How does this connect to who you're becoming?

### Goal Categories

Balance across:

- **Personal**: Health, relationships, growth
- **Professional**: Career, skills, impact
- **Strategic**: Long-term vision, legacy

## Questions

- "What would make this your best year yet?"
- "What are you ready to let go of?"
- "What would the leader you want to be accomplish this year?"
