# Year Summary

Synthesize the year into a comprehensive overview.

- Q1: archived/not archived - [one-line theme]
- Q2: archived/not archived - [one-line theme]
- Q3: archived/not archived - [one-line theme]

Pull one-line summaries from each quarterly archive's synthesis or key theme.

## Energy Trajectory

Analyze energy patterns across the year from quarterly archives:
- Which quarters had highest sustained energy?
- Which quarters showed energy drain or recovery?
- What was the overall annual trajectory: rising, falling, stable, volatile, recovery arc?

Connect energy patterns to major initiatives, life events, or strategic shifts.

## Annual Theme Adherence

Reference the theme established in yearly planning:
- How well did the year align with the intended theme?
- Did the theme evolve or shift during the year?
- What pulled attention away from the theme?
- What reinforced it?

Rate theme adherence: Strong / Moderate / Weak / Evolved

## Major Accomplishments

Aggregate accomplishments across quarters:
- Count major outcomes completed
- List the 3-5 most significant achievements
- Note accomplishments that exceeded expectations
- Identify accomplishments that shifted trajectory

## Key Dates

Review key dates and milestones from the year:
- Which strategic milestones were achieved?
- Which slipped or were abandoned?
- What unexpected significant moments emerged?

## Validation

Present the year summary and ask:
> "Does this feel like an accurate picture of your year?"

Allow corrections before proceeding to deeper reflection.
