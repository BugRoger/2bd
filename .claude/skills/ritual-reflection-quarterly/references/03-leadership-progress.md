# Leadership Progress

Connect quarter results to leadership development trajectory.

## Growth Edge Progress

Reference growth_edge from directives and Year.md.

Explore where it showed up this quarter:
> "Your growth edge is [growth_edge]. Where did that show up this quarter?"

Probe for specific moments:
- Situations where the edge was tested
- Times when you succeeded at the edge
- Times when you fell back into old patterns

Ask: "How much closer are you to moving past this edge?"

## Leadership Identity Embodiment

Reference leadership_identity from directives.

Explore how they showed up as that leader:
> "As someone who [leadership_identity], where did you embody that most strongly?"

Look for:
- Decisions that reflected the identity
- Moments of alignment
- Gaps between identity and action

Ask: "What was your biggest leadership moment this quarter?"

## Avoided Conversations

Review avoided conversations trend across weekly archives.

Identify patterns:
- Which conversations persisted all quarter?
- Which were finally addressed?
- What enabled resolution or continued avoidance?

Ask: "Is there a conversation you're still avoiding that would change things?"

## Self-Care Reflection

Energy sustainability across the quarter:
- What practices emerged that protected energy?
- What caused burnout or depletion?
- What needs to be protected next quarter?

Ask: "What sustainable practice will you commit to next quarter?"
