# Week Summary

Synthesize the week's data into a quantitative overview.

## Gather Metrics

From daily archives and calendar, compute:

### Activity Metrics

- **Days reviewed** — Count of daily archives available (e.g., "4 of 5 workdays")
- **Total meetings** — Sum of meetings across all days
- **Total focus hours** — Sum of focus hours from daily frontmatter
- **Meeting load** — Percentage of work time in meetings

### Energy Metrics

- **Energy trend** — Plot daily energy levels (High/Medium/Low)
- **Average energy** — Overall energy assessment for the week
- **Energy pattern** — Note any day-of-week patterns

### Priority Metrics

- **Priorities set** — Count of priorities across all days
- **Priorities completed** — Count marked done
- **Completion rate** — Percentage completed
- **Carry-forward count** — Priorities that moved between days

## Key Dates Check

Load Quarter.md and review key dates:

### Status Check

- Which key dates passed this week?
- Were deadlines met?
- Any dates requiring attention next week?

Surface upcoming dates from Quarter.md Key Dates section with countdown.

## Present Summary

Show synthesized week overview:

```
Week Summary: YYYY-Www

Days Reviewed: 4/5 workdays
Meetings: 18 total (avg 3.6/day)
Focus Hours: 12.5 total (avg 2.5/day)
Energy Trend: Mon(H) → Tue(M) → Wed(M) → Thu(L) → Fri(M)
Priority Completion: 12/15 (80%)
```

Ask: "Does this feel accurate to your experience of the week?"
