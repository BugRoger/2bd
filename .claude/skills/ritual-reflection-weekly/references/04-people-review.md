# People Review

Synthesize 1:1 meetings and key relationships from the week.

## Gather Interactions

From daily archives and calendar, identify:

### 1:1 Meetings

List all 1:1 meetings held:
- Person name
- Date and duration
- Any notes captured in daily archive

### Significant Interactions

Beyond scheduled 1:1s, note:
- Key conversations mentioned in daily insights
- People referenced in wins
- Relationship moments worth remembering

## Per-Person Reflection

For each key person this week, ask:

### Main Takeaway

"What's your main takeaway from your interaction with [Person]?"

Focus on:
- Their current state/challenges
- Progress on shared goals
- Relationship health signal

### Follow-up Needed

"Is any follow-up needed with [Person]?"

If yes, capture:
- What action is needed
- Timeline/urgency
- Where to track (carry-forward, People file, project)
