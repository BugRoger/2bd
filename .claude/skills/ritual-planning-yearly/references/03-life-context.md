# Life Context

Step back to consider the broader context shaping the year ahead. This grounds vision and goals in reality.

## Explore Through Conversation

Guide the user through these domains:

### Role & Responsibilities

- Any role changes expected this year?
- New scope, team, or reporting structure?
- Shifting priorities from leadership above?
- Career stage or trajectory considerations?

### Team & Organisation

- Team composition changes anticipated?
- Organisational shifts to navigate?
- Strategic direction changes from the business?
- New stakeholders or partnerships?

### Personal

- Life milestones or transitions?
- Health, energy, or lifestyle priorities?
- Learning or growth aspirations beyond work?
- Family, relationships, or life-stage considerations?

### External Factors

- Market or industry shifts relevant to work?
- Economic or business context?
- Any external constraints or opportunities?

## Synthesis

After exploring:

1. Identify the 3-5 most significant contextual factors
2. Note how each might influence the year's direction
3. Surface tensions or tradeoffs to navigate

## Update Year.md

After synthesizing context:

1. Read `00_Brain/Captive/Year.md`
2. Add `## Life Context` section after Prior Synthesis
3. Include:
   - Key contextual factors (bulleted)
   - Implications for year planning
   - Tensions or tradeoffs identified
4. Write entire file back to `00_Brain/Captive/Year.md`

Proceed to Vision.
